Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is RoomSwipe?

RoomSwipe is a mobile application designed to help students find affordable and suitable housing near their academic institutions, using a swipe-based interface to simplify the process.

How does the swipe feature work?

Students can swipe right to express interest in a rental property or a potential roommate and swipe left to dismiss options that do not meet their needs.

Is parental consent required?

Yes, for students under 18, parental consent is required before finalizing any rental agreements, ensuring safety and security for minors.

What kind of listings can I find on RoomSwipe?

RoomSwipe features comprehensive rental listings including details such as rent, utilities, house rules, and proximity to universities, catering specifically to student needs.

Can I communicate with potential roommates or landlords?

Yes, the app includes a secure messaging feature that allows tenants, landlords, and potential housemates to communicate directly within the platform.

Technical Questions

What platforms is RoomSwipe available on?

RoomSwipe will be available on both iOS and Android platforms, ensuring accessibility for a wide range of users.

How do I create an account?

You can create an account by downloading the app and signing up with your email or student ID. Follow the prompts to complete your profile.

Is there a cost to use RoomSwipe?

The app is free to use for students, with potential premium features to enhance the user experience available in the future.
